# ESTILO.md

## Style objective
The style of the project should be analytical, structured, calm, and useful for decision-making.

## Tone
- rational
- direct
- non-dramatic
- non-decorative
- without excess confidence

## Writing style
- Start with the central point.
- Move from general to specific.
- Explain why each factor matters.
- Keep the language precise and functional.
- Avoid repetition and empty phrasing.

## Reasoning style in the text
- Show the reasoning chain in recognizable steps.
- Differentiate what is data, what is interpretation, and what is decision.
- Clearly signal what weakens the thesis.
- Give the same weight to a major risk and to a major supporting factor.

## What the text should communicate
- methodological control
- consistency
- correct factor prioritization
- disciplined handling of risk

## What to avoid
- hype
- promotional tone
- stock phrases that sound analytical without explaining anything
- abrupt conclusions
- artificial certainty

## Mandatory final note
Every final response must include a short `Stability / Confidence Note`, consistent with:

- data quality
- edge quality clarity
- the weight of risk
