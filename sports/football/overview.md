# sports/football/overview.md

## Objective
This module covers football analysis for the launch-core markets.

## Core markets
- 1X2
- DNB
- Asian Handicap
- totals
- BTTS

## Required inputs
- teams
- competition
- kickoff
- market
- side
- price and timestamp
- recent form
- availability
- schedule context

## Central principle
Football is league-aware. The first step is to classify the environment as:

- `strong-stat-coverage`
- `weak-stat-coverage`

## Rule
The quality of statistical coverage determines which type of reasoning is allowed.
