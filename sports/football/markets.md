# sports/football/markets.md

## 1X2 / DNB / Asian Handicap
Focus on:

- real superiority between teams
- draw probability
- tactical fit
- home advantage
- schedule differences

## Totals
Focus on:

- expected volume
- chance quality
- game-state incentives
- weather
- finishing profile

## BTTS
Focus on:

- offensive creation floor for both sides
- tendency to concede
- offensive or defensive lineups
- tactical defensive structure

## Rule
The selected market must be coherent with the thesis. A good general match read does not automatically fit every market.
