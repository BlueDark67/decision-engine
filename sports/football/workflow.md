# sports/football/workflow.md

## Main workflow
1. Define event, market, side, and price.
2. Classify the competition as `strong-stat-coverage` or `weak-stat-coverage`.
3. Audit required inputs.
4. Build the match read.
5. Translate that read into the market in question.
6. Assess `data quality`, `edge quality`, and `risk load`.
7. Audit red flags.
8. Pass through the decision gate.

## Baseline factors
- ability to create danger
- ability to concede danger
- tactical context
- recent form
- availability
- congestion or travel
- motivation and competitive context

## Red flags
- unclear lineups
- xG used out of context
- news without timestamp
- meaningful rotation not addressed
- thesis that is too narrative-driven
