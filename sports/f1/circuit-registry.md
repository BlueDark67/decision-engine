# sports/f1/circuit-registry.md

## Objective
Classify circuits by structural characteristics that change the relative weight of qualifying position, race pace, strategy, and incident volatility.

## High Downforce
- Monaco
- Singapore
- Hungary

Interpretation note:
- Overtaking is very difficult.
- Qualifying position is usually critical.

## Medium Downforce
- Bahrain
- Spain
- Abu Dhabi

## Low Downforce (Power Tracks)
- Monza
- Spa
- Baku

Interpretation note:
- Slipstream overtaking is more common.
- Qualifying position is still relevant but typically less decisive than at high-downforce tracks.

## Street Circuits
- Monaco
- Singapore
- Baku
- Jeddah
- Las Vegas

Interpretation note:
- Safety Car probability is higher.
- Track evolution is significant across sessions.

## High Tyre Deg
- Barcelona
- Bahrain

Interpretation note:
- Tyre-management and pit-stop strategy become critical.

## Registry usage rule
- Use this registry to weight qualifying versus race-pace evidence by circuit profile.
- Combine this registry with active timing mode before assigning edge confidence.
