# sports/nba/props-workflow.md

## Main workflow
1. Define event, player, prop type, side, line, and price.
2. Audit the injury report, projected starters, and rotation context with timestamps.
3. Assess role stability and expected minutes.
4. Build the prop-specific matchup read.
5. Translate that read into the selected over/under market.
6. Assess `data quality`, `edge quality`, and `risk load`.
7. Audit prop red flags.
8. Pass through the decision gate.

## Baseline factors
- expected minutes
- role stability
- usage or touch profile
- pace
- shot profile
- rebound environment
- assist environment
- rest and travel
- blowout risk

## Rule
Do not let a recent streak replace clear minutes, role, and matchup logic.
