# sports/nba/markets.md

## Moneyline
Use when the overall advantage exists and the price still compensates for it.

## Spread
Focus on:

- matchup edge
- rotation and availability
- rest and travel
- reliability of the closing unit

## Game Total
Focus on:

- pace
- efficiency
- shot profile
- impact of absences on style
- foul and endgame inflation risk

## Rule
Do not project a spread edge automatically onto the total, or the reverse.
