# sports/nba/restricted-secondary-scope.md

## Props
NBA props remain outside the NBA launch core, but are now supported through a restricted secondary module.

## Supported markets
- over/under `PTS`
- over/under `AST`
- over/under `REB`
- over/under `PA`
- over/under `PR`
- over/under `PRA`
- over/under `3PM`

## Status
- secondary scope
- restricted use
- dedicated workflow required

## Core restriction
Do not treat props as interchangeable with game markets.

## Promotion rule
Do not promote props as normal workflow unless:

- minutes are sufficiently stable
- role is clear
- injury context is confirmed
- the prop family fits the matchup thesis
- the current line still preserves edge

## Special caution
- combo props must not hide a weak component
- made-threes props carry higher variance than `PTS`, `AST`, or `REB`
