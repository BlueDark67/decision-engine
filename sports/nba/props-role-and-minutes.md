# sports/nba/props-role-and-minutes.md

## Objective
Define the role and minutes discipline required for NBA player props.

## Core checks
- expected starting status
- recent minute range
- any minutes cap or recent return from injury
- role in the first and closing units
- usage or touch concentration
- teammate absences or returns that change the role

## Practical rules
- stable minutes increase support for props
- unstable minutes sharply raise `risk load`
- unclear role lowers `data quality`
- injury-driven role spikes need timestamped confirmation

## Promotion rule
Props should rarely reach `enter` when:

- minutes are not stable
- the player is on restriction
- the role depends on unresolved injury news
- the game script can destroy the player pathway too easily
