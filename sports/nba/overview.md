# sports/nba/overview.md

## Objective
This module covers NBA analysis for the launch core.

## Core markets
- moneyline
- spread
- game total

## Restricted secondary scope
- props

## Required inputs
- teams
- tipoff
- market
- side
- price and timestamp
- injury report with timestamp
- rotation context
- rest and travel
- form and matchup

## Central principle
At launch, the NBA read should be driven by availability, rotation, rest, travel, and matchup, not by loose narratives about momentum.
