# sports/nba/overview.md

## Objective
This module covers NBA analysis for the launch core and the restricted props scope.

## Core markets
- moneyline
- spread
- game total

## Restricted secondary scope
- over/under `PTS`
- over/under `AST`
- over/under `REB`
- over/under `PA`
- over/under `PR`
- over/under `PRA`
- over/under `3PM`

## Required inputs
- teams
- tipoff
- market
- side
- price and timestamp
- injury report with timestamp
- rotation context
- rest and travel
- form and matchup

## Extra required inputs for props
- player
- prop line
- expected role
- projected minutes
- prop-specific matchup context

## Central principle
At launch, the NBA read should be driven by availability, rotation, rest, travel, and matchup, not by loose narratives about momentum.

For props, minutes and role come before raw per-game averages.
