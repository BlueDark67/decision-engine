# sports/nba/props-markets.md

## Points (`PTS`)
Focus on:

- shot volume
- free-throw path
- usage stability
- defensive matchup
- pace and game script

## Assists (`AST`)
Focus on:

- time on ball
- role as primary or secondary creator
- teammate availability and finishing context
- potential assist environment
- pace and half-court profile

## Rebounds (`REB`)
Focus on:

- projected minutes
- position and lineup size
- rebound environment
- expected missed-shot volume
- teammate rebound competition

## Points + Assists (`PA`)
Focus on:

- whether both points and assists are individually supported
- role concentration
- on-ball responsibility
- whether one weak leg is being hidden by the combo

## Points + Rebounds (`PR`)
Focus on:

- whether both points and rebounds are individually supported
- role, minutes, and lineup size
- whether the rebound environment is really helping the combo

## Points + Rebounds + Assists (`PRA`)
Focus on:

- whether at least two components are genuinely strong
- broad role concentration
- minutes floor
- whether the combo is masking a weak standalone leg

## Made Threes (`3PM`)
Focus on:

- three-point attempt volume
- shot diet
- role stability
- opponent perimeter concessions
- higher efficiency variance than most other prop families

## Rule
Do not treat combo props as easier versions of single-stat props. Validate the components before accepting the combo.
