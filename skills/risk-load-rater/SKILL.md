# Risk Load Rater

Read `../../core/risk-load.md`, `../../core/red-flags.md`, and the relevant sport workflow.

## Workflow
1. Measure how dependent the thesis is on unstable inputs.
2. Identify contextual volatility and contradiction risk.
3. Rate the setup as `low`, `medium`, `high`, or `extreme`.

## Return
- risk load rating
- main risk drivers
- note on whether the risk is manageable or thesis-breaking
